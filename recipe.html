Beef tacos

Ingredients Directions
Chuck roast Pre Heat oven 325
Vegetable oil Cut boneless beef into chuncks
Beef Broth Heat oil in skillet and sear seasoned beef, half at a time, until browned on all sides
Chipotle chilis Pulse together chipotle chilies, garlic and 1/4 cup beef broth until pureed
Garlic Whisk together remaining beef broth with chipotle mixture, cumin, oregano, salt, pepper and cloves
Cumin, cloves, oregano, bay leaves (as needed to add flavor) Pour mixture over beef in slow cooker, add bay leaves.
Salt & pepper (season with fair amount so it doesn't taste flat) Cover and cook on low heat 8 – 9 hours. In crockpot
Fresh lime juice Remove beef from slow cooker (leave broth) and shred.
--------- Stir lime juice into broth in slow cooker then return beef to slow cooker and cook on low or warm 20 – 30 minutes longer.

Hamburger

Ingredients Directions
1 egg Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
1/2 teaspoon salt In a medium bowl, whisk together egg, salt and pepper.
1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper Place ground beef and bread crumbs into the mixture.
1 pound ground beef With hands or a fork, mix until well blended
1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs Form into 4 patties approximately 3/4 inch thick.
------------------ Place patties on the prepared grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
